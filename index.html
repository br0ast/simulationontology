HyperReal

HyperReal is the first Knowledge Graph completely dedicated to cultural symbolism. It contains more than 40,000 instances of symbol-symbolic meaning relationships, also called Simulations. It follows the structure of the Simulation Ontology. For more information about it you can read a paper that describes it here! To cite it:

Bruno Sartini, Marieke van Erp, and Aldo Gangemi. Marriage is a peach and a chalice: Modelling cultural symbolism on the semantic web. In Proceedings of the 11th on Knowledge Capture Conference, K-CAP ’21, page 201–208, New York, NY, USA, 2021. Association for Computing Machinery.
